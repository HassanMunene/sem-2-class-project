About us

Being students and going through the daily up and downs of trying to
fit in a university society that is rapidly changing especially in our social
life and that concerns what we wear on our daily basis we decided to team
up as a group of 10 members and establish a small retail shop to sell sneakers
at a favourable price and in the process help our fellow students stand out too.
and thats how KCAsneakers was born.
